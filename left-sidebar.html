About me

My name is Kyle Pinca, and I am a passionate and detail-oriented data analyst with a strong background in extracting valuable insights from complex datasets. I thrive on the challenges that data presents and enjoy transforming raw information into actionable strategies

I hold a B.A. in Management Information Systems with a minor in Business Analytics from Western Washington University. There I gained a solid foundation in statistical analysis, data mining, and data visualization techniques. During my studies, I worked on several projects that involved cleaning, analyzing, and interpreting data, which allowed me to develop a keen eye for accuracy and a deep understanding of analytics

After completing my degree,I furthered my education by acquiring the Google Data Analytics certification, where I honed my skills in manipulating and analyzing large datasets. I leveraged various tools and programming languages such as SQL, Python, and R to extract meaningful insights and provide data-driven recommendations.
